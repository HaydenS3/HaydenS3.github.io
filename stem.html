STEM Outreach
Since I joined my robotics team I've been very active in STEM Outreach. I love getting young minds intrested and engaged in STEM, and it's amazing to see faces light up in the same way mine did when I first discovered robotics. Some outreach events I've volunteered for include: refreeing for FLL competitions, shooting T-Shirts out of our robot, Reggie, at football games, mentoring for LEGO and VEX Robotics camps, and constructing beeping easter eggs for a blind easter egg hunt.
