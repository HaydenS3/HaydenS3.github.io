My freshman year I received the position of Software and Control Systems lead. When I first started in the position I had a lot to learn about leading a team, but as the years have passed I have grown into a skilled leader. During the offseason I teach my crew in Java using a curriculum I built myself. During the season I delegate tasks and maintain communication with other crews while defining a road map for the future. I try to give my crew the freedom to learn and grow on their own by stepping back while continuing to provide guidance and resources to them when in need.
